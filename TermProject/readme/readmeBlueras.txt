|GAME DESCRIPTION|

You have 2 challenges in this game both of which are displayed on the left side of the game but will be repeated below.

1. Dodge the incoming obstacles

2. Keep Mr.BlueRasberry alive!

These are the main idea of the game for a competitive aspect share with a friend and see who can score the most points!
